By accessing or using our Platform, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

We collect information necessary to provide, improve, and secure our services. This includes:

  • Business Information: Company name, tax ID, business address, and contact details (email, phone number).

  • Transactional Data: Details of international payments, currency conversions, and recipient information.
  • Technical Data: IP address, device information, browser type, and usage patterns (e.g., via cookies and analytics tools).
  • User Credentials: Username, password, and authentication details for Platform access.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Analyze trends to optimize payment solutions and business operations.
  • Prevent fraud, unauthorized access, or misuse of the Platform.
